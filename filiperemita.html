About Me

Meta Ads specialist · AI prompt engineer · Creative lead · Growth marketer. I'm Filip Eremita, based in Rečica ob Savinji, Slovenia.

I'm a digital marketing and conversion optimisation specialist with a strong technical and analytical background. My passion is finding the intersection of data and creativity — from designing high-converting Meta Ads campaigns and UX/UI landing page audits, to independent web development and data analytics. I approach every project holistically: I understand the psychology of purchase through Direct Response copywriting as well as the technical stack behind web analytics and data tracking.

Currently at 2Digit d.o.o. — managing large-scale Meta campaigns across Italy, Hungary, Poland, and Czechia, with 7-figure monthly ad spend managed profitably. Proven track record with Advantage+ Shopping vs. CBO/ABO architecture, Pixel & CAPI implementation, Lookalike scaling, and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).

I work AI-first: Sora, Veo3.1, Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT are daily production tools. I also vibe code — building custom dashboards, automations, and tools (HTML, CSS, JS, GitHub Pages) when off-the-shelf doesn't cut it.

Meta Ads Expertise

A Senior/Expert-level breakdown of my Meta Ads competencies across four pillars — strategy, creative, technical tracking, and post-click optimisation. The best media buyers don't just click around Ads Manager. They master psychology, technology, and data simultaneously.

01 ——
Media Buying
& Strategy
Full-funnel architecture · Scaling · Data-driven decisions
Full-Funnel Architecture
TOFU → MOFU → BOFU campaign design. Different messaging for cold, warm, and hot audiences. Retargeting sequences that compound over time.
Budget Allocation & Scaling
CBO/ABO campaign structures, vertical and horizontal scaling strategies — while maintaining or improving target ROAS and CPA. Managing 7-figure monthly budgets profitably.
Data-Driven Decision Making
Real-time analysis of CTR, CPM, CPC, Hook Rate, Hold Rate. Fast identification of winners, ruthless cutting of losers. No emotional attachment to creatives — only metrics.
02 ——
Creative Strategy
& Production
Angles · Hooks · Video prompts · DR Copywriting
Angle & Hook Development
Systematic development and A/B testing of sales angles — problem demonstration with visual proof, health awareness funnels, lifestyle transformation narratives. The hook is the targeting.
Video Prompt Writing
Precise briefs for AI video production — tone, visual layout, per-second scenarios. First 3 seconds engineered for scroll-stopping. Hook rate optimisation as a KPI.
Direct Response Copywriting
Hook → Story/Educate → Offer → CTA framework. Multilingual (EN, IT, PL, SL, HU, CZ, SK) copy adapted to product, market, and funnel stage. Pain-point first, always.
03 ——
Technical Meta
Marketing
Pixel · CAPI · QA · First-party data
Advanced Tracking Implementation
End-to-end data flow between e-commerce store and Meta. Custom Event setup and debugging. Because of my HTML/CSS/JS background, I can read and fix tracking code directly — no intermediary needed.
Conversions API (CAPI)
Server-side tracking for accurate attribution and first-party data collection in the post-iOS 14 privacy era. Understanding of event deduplication and EMQ (Event Match Quality) optimisation.
Quality Assurance (QA)
Technical review of tracking implementation at code level. Dramatically reduces conversion reporting errors. A technical edge most marketers don't have.
04 ——
Post-Click
Optimisation
CRO · UX/UI · Message Match · A/B
Landing Page Audits
Structured reviews identifying friction points in the purchase flow. UX/UI improvement recommendations backed by campaign data — not guesswork. The ad gets the click; the page makes the sale.
Message Match
Perfect alignment between Meta ad visual/copy and landing page content. Same promise, same design language, same offer. Reduces bounce rate and increases CR measurably.
A/B Testing Landing Pages
Data-driven page iteration: buttons, layouts, product descriptions, hero sections. Changes proposed and implemented based on campaign performance signals, not assumptions.
